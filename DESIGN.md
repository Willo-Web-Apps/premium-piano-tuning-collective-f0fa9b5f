# Design DNA — Clavara

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: storytelling
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Piano Lacquer): #0A0A0A — `oklch(0.09 0.005 60)`
- Accent (Concert Gold): #C9A84C — `oklch(0.72 0.12 78)`
- Surface Dark (Charcoal): #111111 — `oklch(0.12 0.006 60)`
- Surface Light (Ivory/Parchment): #F5F0E8 — `oklch(0.96 0.015 80)`
- Body Text: warm ivory — `oklch(0.88 0.018 75)`

### Font Pairing
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — weights 300, 400, 600, 700 (dramatic, luxury editorial)
- Body: Montserrat — weights 300, 400, 500 (refined, precise)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Full-dark storytelling canvas** — near-black backgrounds throughout, alternating between #0A0A0A and #111111 charcoal. No light sections ever appear, reinforcing the concert-grand lacquer aesthetic.
2. **Gold hairline dividers** — 1px champagne gold horizontal rules separate every section, evoking the brass fixtures of great concert halls. Used sparingly as a signature accent.
3. **Dramatic typographic scale** — Hero headline in Cormorant Garamond at 8xl–9xl with ultra-light tracking, creating the gravitas of Carnegie Hall opening night. Body copy at generous sizes to ensure readable luxury, not information density.
4. **Editorial service presentation** — Services listed as an elegant vertical roster with gold number indices and spaced rules — no card grids, no icon grids. Like a program printed for an opening-night performance.
5. **Noise texture overlay** — Subtle grain on the hero and dark sections to evoke the feel of concert hall upholstery and prevent the "too-digital" look.
